About Our College

Our History

Founded in 1985, Government Engineering College Barmer has been a pioneer in providing quality education for over three decades. We've grown from a small institution to a leading educational hub with state-of-the-art facilities.

Our Mission

To empower students with knowledge, skills, and values that prepare them for global challenges. We foster innovation, critical thinking, and social responsibility in a diverse learning environment.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier institution that transforms lives through education, research, and community engagement, producing leaders who make a positive impact on society.

Admissions Process

Begin your journey at Modern College with our straightforward admission process.

B Tech Admissions

1

Check Eligibility

Minimum 60% in 10+2 or equivalent examination from a recognized board.

2

Submit Application

Complete the online application form and pay the application fee.

3

Entrance Test

Appear for the Modern College Entrance Test (MCET) or submit valid national test scores.

4

Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be called for a personal interview.

5

Admission Offer

Successful candidates will receive admission offers via email.
